About me – FAQs

Can you give us an introduction to yourself? +

Hey, I’m Fred. Originally from Bath, I’ve lived in London for two years with my partner, Tamara.

What are your hobbies? +

I’m all about running, football, early mornings and meditation, which I know is some people’s idea of living hell. Excluding football, these activities offer me times of reflection and are invaluable to my life.

Football... well, it allows me to be irrationally passionate for a couple of hours a week over something very trivial.

Do you have a degree? +

I do. I originally studied Graphic Design in Southampton. The design world was beautiful (websites, logos, typography), and the detail-orientated nature was relatable, but I never connected with the design craft. Designing never brought the joy I thought it would.

This led me to pursue a master’s degree at the University of Bath in Management, with my thinking being, “Hey, if I don’t enjoy actually designing, maybe I will enjoy managing design projects”.

This hypothesis was partly true. I like it, but it isn’t quite that final bowl of Goldilock’s porridge.

Are you currently working? +

I am a project manager for a design agency specialising in creating websites, digital products and brands.

It was here that I first encountered the world of web development. Working daily with web developers opened my eyes to a then mysterious craft.
